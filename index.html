Cold Tea!

An online face synthesis and compare service by taking pictures immediately.

How to Play?

1. Take pictures

Click the 'Snap!' button to take a picture of yourself and the result will be shown on the left.

2. Drag & Drop

Drag the picture between the two lists and make sure every list has at least one snapshot.

3. Face Synthesis & Compare Similarity

Click 'Compare' or 'Synthesize' to see the result.

First image in the two lists will be used.
